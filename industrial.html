A Passion for Automation

Kingdom Control Solutions is an independent controls engineering and consulting firm specializing in industrial automation. We bring deep hands-on experience to complex control challenges — from first-principles design through commissioning and startup.

We offer consulting and engineering services across the full project lifecycle: system architecture, PLC and HMI programming, safety system design, SCADA development, and on-site commissioning support. Kingdom's Principals

Three Pillars of Focus

Safety
Safety is our top priority. Your most valuable assets are your people. We provide controls that prioritize their safety through tested and proven SIL rated hardware and robust and redundant programming, making sure they go home safe every day.
Runtime
Production is why you are in business. Our controls help you stay running by optimizing equipment efficiency, are intuitive enough to help you work through equipment faults quickly, and tuned enough to run tight control, helping you produce high quality products on time every time.
Longevity
Equipment is built to last, so should your controls. Whatever your environment is we make sure to engineer systems to the required spec, giving the most critical part of your system the defense it needs against harsh conditions.
